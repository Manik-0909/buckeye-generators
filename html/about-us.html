About
Buckeye Generators

Company Overview

Buckeye Generators is a certified DBA business specializing in the sales, installation, and servicing of Generac generators for homes and businesses. We proudly serve Geauga County, Ashtabula County, and surrounding areas, providing dependable backup power solutions tailored to your needs.

As a licensed and trained Generac installer, our team ensures that every generator is installed safely, efficiently, and in full compliance with local codes. From initial consultation to routine maintenance, we offer complete, turnkey service to keep your property powered through any outage.

We support residential, commercial, and light industrial clients with 24/7 emergency support, so you're never left in the dark. Whether you're securing your home, office, or essential facility, Buckeye Generators is committed to delivering peace of mind with every install.

Trust Buckeye Generators—your certified Generac partner for reliable backup power.
